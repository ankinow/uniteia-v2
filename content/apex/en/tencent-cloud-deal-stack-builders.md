# Tencent Cloud Deal Stack: Cheap Cloud for Builders

## What is the Tencent Cloud Deal Stack?

Tencent Cloud offers a range of products and promotions ideal for independent builders. With Lighthouse, CVM, and EdgeOne, you can host sites, APIs, bots, dashboards, and small apps spending very little — in some cases, nothing.

## Free Products

Tencent Cloud provides a free tier with select products. Free resources include:

- **EdgeOne:** Up to 1M requests/month on the free plan
- **Lighthouse:** Trial period on basic configurations
- **CVM:** Promotional offers for new users

> ⚠️ **Note:** Free products may require credit card registration. Check official terms.

## Lighthouse — Simplicity That Works

Lighthouse is a simplified VPS, ideal for those who don't want to manage complex infrastructure.

**When to use:**
- Static sites or blogs
- Lightweight APIs
- Bots and automation
- Dev environments
- Personal dashboards

**Pros:**
- Fixed specs, no surprises
- Simplified dashboard
- Firewall and monitoring included
- Cheaper than equivalent CVM
- Monthly or hourly billing

## CVM — Full Power

CVM (Cloud Virtual Machine) is the complete solution for those needing full control.

**When to use:**
- CPU/RAM-intensive applications
- Kubernetes or advanced Docker
- Custom kernel or network tuning
- Large databases
- Environments needing VPC and security groups

**Pros:**
- Fully customizable configuration
- Dedicated, spot, and reserved instances
- Additional block storage
- Per-second billing (1-hour minimum)
- BYOL support

## EdgeOne — CDN + Security

EdgeOne combines CDN with WAF, DDoS protection, and bot management in one platform.

**When to use:**
- Accelerate global content delivery
- Protect sites against attacks
- Replace separate CDN + WAF
- Reduce latency for international users

**Pros:**
- Generous free tier (1M req/month)
- Pay-as-you-go
- Global edge network
- Native integration with Lighthouse and CVM
- No complex licensing

## How to Combine Products

| Stack | Lighthouse + EdgeOne | CVM + EdgeOne |
|-------|---------------------|---------------|
| Best for | Sites, blogs, landing pages | Dynamic apps, APIs, e-commerce |
| Performance | Great for static content | Maximum flexibility |
| Cost | Lowest | Moderate |
| Setup | Minutes | Hours |

## Before You Pay — Checklist

1. **Check region:** Not all promos are available in all regions
2. **Eligibility:** Some offers are new users only
3. **Validity:** Promotions expire — check the date
4. **Renewal:** Promo price may not apply on renewal
5. **Coupons:** Read terms before activating — some require minimum spend
6. **Free tier:** Confirm whether credit card is required
